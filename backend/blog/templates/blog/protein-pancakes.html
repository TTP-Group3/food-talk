Protein Pancakes

What you feed your body in the morning will determine the type day you have. This quick, easy, and delicious protein pancake recipe will ensure you start of the day with the right amount of nutrients to power your day.

    Ingredients

    • 4 eggs
    • 1 Banana
    • 1 Cup of Oats
    • 1 Scoop Protein Powder (Vanilla or Chocolate)
    • Handfull of Blueberries
    • Pancake Syrup
    • 1 tablespoon of Flaxseeds (optional)
